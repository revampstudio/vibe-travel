## Tester Options

Option 1: Play Console email list.

- Add individual tester Google-account email addresses directly in Play Console.
- This is simplest if the tester list is mostly fixed.

Option 2: Google Group.

- Create a Google Group such as `Vibe Travel Testers`.
- Add the Google Group to Play Console as the tester source.
- This is easier if testers will be added over time.

For either option, Google production access requires:

- A closed testing release published.
- At least 12 testers opted in.
- The closed test running with at least 12 opted-in testers for at least 14 days.
- After that, apply for production access in Play Console.
